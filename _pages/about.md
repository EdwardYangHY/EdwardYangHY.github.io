I am Haoyuan Yang, a Ph.D. student at Center for Robust Speech Systems (CRSS) at UT Dallas, under supervision of John H.L. Hansen.

My work focuses on Speech Generation, Emotional and Expressive Voice Conversion.

I received my M.S. in Engineering from Tokyo Tech in 2024. Before that, I studied mathematics in Southease University, Nanjing, China.

Research Interests
======
- Speech Generation
- Emotion in Speech
- Audio-Visual Multimodality

Current Projects
======
- **Emotional Voice Conversion**: Focusing on transfering the Emotional State of an utterance while maintaining the linguistic contents, speaker ID and overall quality.
- **Speaker Anonymization**: Focusing on removing speaker-specific identity while keep the linguistic contents for privacy concerns.
